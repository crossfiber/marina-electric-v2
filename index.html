Process · Five stages

How a marina electrical project actually moves.

Every project runs the same five-stage loop, whether it's a single pedestal swap or a full resort substation rebuild. Same engineering rigor either way.

01

Site Survey

Voltage, resistance, polarity & ground integrity testing. We measure what's there before we quote.

02

Design & Drawings

In-house electrical drawings, load calcs, single-line diagrams. Nothing gets bid without them.

03

Value Engineering

Budgeting, estimating, code-compliance review — we cut waste before it reaches the dock.

04

Installation

Steel works, cable trays, stainless penetrations, waterproof splices. Built for saltwater.

05

Commission & Maintain

Commissioning, data power logs, preventive maintenance, 24/7 emergency response.
