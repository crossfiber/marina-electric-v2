Why Marina Electric

An electrical partner that knows the water — and the rest of the trade too.

Every project runs the same five-step loop — whether it's a single pedestal swap or a full resort substation rebuild. You get the same engineering rigor either way.

01

Site Survey

Voltage, resistance, polarity & ground integrity testing. We measure what's there before we quote.

02

Design & Drawings

In-house electrical drawings, load calcs, single-line diagrams. Nothing gets bid without them.

03

Value Engineering

Budgeting, estimating, code-compliance review — we cut waste before it reaches the dock.

04

Installation

Commission & Maintain
